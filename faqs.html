Expedition Blueprint

Critical Travel Insights, Hunting Regulations, and Lodge Directives for Global Patrons

General Travel Questions

Where is Lanyonvale Safaris located?
Lanyonvale Safaris is located on Farm Spioenkop in the Northern Cape, South Africa. It is a true Karoo hunting destination with open plains, big skies, and authentic African wilderness.
Do I need a visa to visit South Africa?
Most international guests, including U.S. citizens, do not require a visa for stays under 90 days. A valid passport with at least two blank pages is required.
Is airport transfer available?
Yes, we can arrange transfers from nearby airports such as Upington or Kimberley upon request.

Hunting & Safari Questions

Can I bring my own rifle?
Yes. We assist with the SAPS 520 temporary firearm import permit process and guide you step-by-step before arrival.
What animals can I hunt?
We offer a wide range of Karoo plains game including kudu, gemsbok, springbok, blesbok, impala, wildebeest, zebra, and more.
Do I need hunting experience?
No experience is required. Our professional hunters guide beginners and experienced hunters alike with full safety and ethical standards.
Is this a fair-chase hunting environment?
Yes. All hunts are conducted ethically in a natural, free-range environment with respect for wildlife and conservation principles.

Lodge & Accommodation

What is included in the safari package?
Accommodation, meals, soft drinks, hunting vehicle during activities, professional guiding, and basic lodge services are included unless otherwise stated.
Can non-hunters join the trip?
Yes. We welcome family members, photographers, and observers who want to enjoy the Karoo experience.
Is WiFi available at the lodge?
Yes, WiFi is available at the lodge area. However, we encourage guests to enjoy the peaceful Karoo environment and disconnect when possible.

Booking & Payments

Do I need to pay a deposit?
Yes, a deposit is required to confirm your booking. The remaining balance is payable before or upon arrival.
Can prices change?
Prices may vary depending on season and availability, but confirmed bookings remain locked at the agreed rate.
