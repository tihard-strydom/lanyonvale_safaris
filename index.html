Welcome to Lanyonvale Safaris

Nestled between the historic towns of Griekwastad and Prieska in the vast and untouched landscapes of the South African Karoo, Lanyonvale Safaris is a third-generation hunting farm surrounded by rugged beauty, endless horizons, and true Northern Cape hospitality.

Here, golden sunsets stretch across the open veld, wildlife roams freely, and every moment reflects the raw and peaceful spirit of Africa. Just like the land itself, Lanyonvale offers an experience that is both authentic and unforgettable.

About Us

Lanyonvale Safaris

At Lanyonvale Safaris, we believe that a great hunt is more than just a trophy — it's about the full experience. Whether you're a seasoned hunter or visiting Africa for the first time, our team is committed to providing you with a professional, personalized, and unforgettable safari.

Set in the heart of the Karoo between Griekwastad and Prieska, our farm offers an authentic African hunting environment where ethical hunting, conservation, and respect for nature come first.

From plains game hunts across open Karoo veld to peaceful evenings under star-filled skies, every experience is designed to reflect the true spirit of the Northern Cape and create lifelong memories.

Let Lanyonvale Safaris be your gateway to the African wilderness — where tradition meets excellence, and every hunt becomes a story worth telling.

Meet Your PH

Franco Cilliers

Franco Cilliers is the Professional Hunter at Lanyonvale Safaris, guiding clients through the vast and rugged Karoo landscape between Griekwastad and Prieska. With a deep respect for wildlife, ethical hunting, and the traditions of the African bush, he brings both skill and calm leadership to every safari experience.

Raised with a strong passion for the outdoors and hunting culture, Franco is known for his patience in the field, attention to detail, and ability to make every hunt both safe and memorable. Whether tracking plains game through open veld or sharing stories around the fire, he ensures every guest experiences the true spirit of the Northern Cape wilderness.
