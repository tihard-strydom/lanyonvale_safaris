Welcome to Lanyonvale

Tradition Meets Unrivaled Excellence

Nestled beautifully between the historic milestones of Griekwastad and Prieska within the boundless expanses of the Northern Cape, Lanyonvale Safaris represents a prestigious third-generation tracking territory defined by rugged, raw valleys, timeless horizons, and premier South African hospitality.

At Lanyonvale Safaris, we fundamentally believe that an elite hunt transcends the acquisition of a world-class trophy—it is defined by the full corporate and personal immersion. Whether you are a lifelong dangerous game pursuer or tracing African soil for the absolute first time, our specialized team guarantees a meticulously curated, safe, and elite wilderness venture.

Meet Your Professional Hunter

Franco Cilliers

As the Lead Professional Hunter (PH) at Lanyonvale Safaris, Franco Cilliers masterfully orchestrates all field maneuvers across our extensive Karoo domain. Guided by an unyielding reverence for conservation ethics, pristine wildlife management, and the traditional heritages of African fair-chase tracking, he delivers sophisticated, calm, and highly advanced tactical guidance.

Franco is globally recognized for his profound patience under pressure, impeccable field preparation, and a calculated approach that converts complex stalks into historic tales told around evening fires. Under his watchful care, your safety and pursuit standards remain absolute.
